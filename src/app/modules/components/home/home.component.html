How it works?
An event is a composition of multiple bubble. Each bubble has a type, to provide specific help in your organization. In a bubble, everyone participates and can contribute.
Our bubbles

Click on each one to see details

Alimentation bubble

Indicate what you plan to bring, ask participants to bring some drinks and food. Manage supplies for the event to make sure you don't miss out on anything!

Checkbox bubble

Make a list of things to do, to plan, to organize for the event. Participants will be able to check the elements of the list.

Planning bubble

Organize your planning for your event. Specify what you want to do and when.

Travel bubble

Help participants organize their travel to the event! Some people will be able to offer seats in their cars, and others will be able to ask for them to be picked up!

Free bubble

Organize your planning for your event. Specify what you want to do and when.

Survey bubble

Conduct a survey to solicit feedback from participants.
